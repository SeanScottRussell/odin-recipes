Currie's Curry!

Decription:

A curry recipe handed down by an official Currie, it's a Stephen Currie Curry. Whether you're making chicken, beef, limb or king prawn this curry recipe is perfect.

Strap on your curry making boots and stick your toilet paper in the freezer cause this dish has a kick.

Ingredients:

  • Clarified butter
  • Tin of tomatoes
  • Meat of choice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Corriander
  • Tumeric
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ginger
  • Stock (depending on what meat you're using)
  • Chillies

Method:

  1. In a pot over medium high heat, par cook your meat in the clarified butter. While this is happening dice the onion and mince the garlic and chillies

  2. Once the meat has been par cooked, remove and sit to side. Fry the diced onion in the same pot adding the garlic and chillies when the onion is transulcent.

  3. Add the dry ingredients to the pot and mix until an almost paste is formed then add the tomatoes.

  4. Add the meat back into the pot along with some water and the stock. Then cook down until meat is cook through and desired curry consistancy is achieved.

  5. Serve with rice and/or naan bread.

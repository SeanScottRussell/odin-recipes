Sean's Chilli!

Description:

A delicious beef chilli guranteed to please your girlfriend (if her name is Leanne). It has everything a good chilli needs spice, beef, taste.

So hold on to your kidney beans cause we're going to Chilli Town, population: Flavour!

Ingredients:

I don't really use proper measurements for this recipe so just go easy and don't kick the arse out of them.

  • Chilli oil (normal oil can be used)
  • 5% beef mince
  • Tin of tomatoes
  • Tin of kidney beans
  • 1 onion
  • 2/3 garlic cloves
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cumin
  • cayene
  • chilli flakes
  • chilli powder
  • garlic powder
  • onion granules
  • oregano
  • paprika
  • beef stock cube
  • cocoa powder
  • chipotle paste

Method:

  1. Heat the oil in a medium sized pot. Dice the onion and finely mince the garlic. Add the diced onion to the pot and cook until transulcent and fragrent then add the garlic.

  2. Add the mince to the pot and cook until no pink meat is left.

  3. Add all the dry ingredients (except the stock cube, cocoa powder and chipotle paste) and mix through.

  4. Add the tin of tomatoes and let cook for 5 mins. Then add the stock cube, cocoa powder and chipotle paste, thoroughly stir then add the kidney beans.

  5. Turn temperature down and let cook until desired chilli consistancy is achieved (anywhere between 30mins and 2 hours). In my personal opinion the longer it cooks the tastier it is.

  6. Serve with rice and/or tortilla chips.
